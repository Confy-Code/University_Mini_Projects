## 1. INSURANCE CLAIM HANDLING
Based on the scenario below, create a `Business Process Model and Notation (BPMN) diagram` that represents the motor insurance claim handling process.
The process begins when a customer submits a motor insurance claim together with the required supporting documents. **The Notification Department** at the insurance company reviews the submitted documents for completeness and registers the claim.
Once the claim is registered, the Handling Department receives the claim, verifies the insurance details, and performs an assessment.
After the assessment, a decision is made:
- If the assessment is approved, the garage is contacted to authorize the repairs, and the payment is scheduled in that order.
- If the assessment is not approved, the claim is rejected. 
In both cases, whether the claim is approved or rejected, an outcome letter is sent to the customer, and the process ends.
